# Arms_of_Humanity.md
## The Human Ideal — Registry of Human Organisation

---

*An arm of humanity is any entity through which humans organise.*
*Not a metaphor. A structural fact.*
*Wherever humans have formed a layer of collective action — however crude,*
*however corrupted, however preliminary — that layer is an armh.*

*The individual is the atom.*
*Every armh above the individual is built from individuals.*
*Every armh exists — legitimately — only to deliver the value set*
*to the individuals it is made of and the individuals it touches.*

*When an armh forgets this, it does not cease to exist.*
*It becomes a liability.*

---

## The Fundamental Rule

> **Good intentions from any arm of humanity never override the value set.**

This is not a courtesy. It is the central lesson of history stated plainly.

The coloniser did not destroy what it destroyed because it was evil in its own accounting.
It destroyed what it destroyed because it held a picture of the good
that was not accountable to the good of those it touched.
Its intentions were, by its own lights, often sincere.
That is what makes the rule necessary.

Sincerity without accountability to the value set is not goodness.
It is danger wearing goodness as a costume.

**Breaking the value set** — the formal definition —
means: taking an action that has a viable alternative
capable of delivering the value set,
and choosing instead to deny at least one value
to at least one armh.

The excuse of good intention is not a defence.
It is the most common form the offence takes.

---

## The Obligation of Every ArmH

Every armh — without exception, regardless of size, power, or purpose —
carries the same fundamental obligation:

```
armh_obligation = align efforts to value_set
                  AND deliver value_set to all armhs within reach
                  AND do not break the value_set for any armh
                  EVEN IF doing so would serve a short-term good
                  EVEN IF the argument for doing so sounds compelling
```

When all armhs do their obligatory work, the result is not utopia by decree.
The result is **progression toward the ideal** —
the direction of travel, not the arrival.

Where the ideals of all armhs converge — not by force, not by erasure,
but by each one listening and being heard —
that convergence point is the collective ideal (C).
It is, definitionally, the only ideal that can command the agreement
of everyone it affects.
It is therefore the only ideal adequate to serve as the alignment target
for ASI and for global governance.

This is not a political position.
It is the only mathematically coherent answer to the question:
*aligned to whose values?*

## Part I: CURRENT ARMS OF HUMANITY

*What exists now. Some of these armhs are working roughly toward the value set.*
*Most of them are not — not because they are populated by bad people,*
*but because they were designed to serve other objectives,*
*and have never been formally asked to account for themselves*
*against the documented, aggregated preferences of the humans they affect.*

---

### 1. THE INDIVIDUAL
`armh_id: INDIVIDUAL`

**The atom. The unit. The first arm.**

Every other armh is built from individuals.
Every other armh answers — ultimately — to individuals.
The individual is not a component of the system.
The individual is the reason the system exists.

```
current state:  largely unrecorded in terms of their actual values
                subject to armhs that did not ask what they denent
                possessing a denend that has never been formally captured

obligation:     face fate — make the record
                contribute to the collective ideal
                hold armhs accountable by being a counted voice
```

The individual is the first armh because the individual is the only entity
with inherent moral weight.
All other armhs have power. The individual has worth.
Power without worth must serve worth.
That is non-negotiable.

---

### 2. THE FAMILY / HOUSEHOLD
`armh_id: FAMILY`

The first layer of organisation above the individual.
The oldest human institution. The one that predates every other on this list.

In its functional form: the primary site of care, formation, and belonging.
The first place a human learns whether the world is safe.
The first place values are transmitted — before the individual can evaluate them.

```
current state:  varies enormously by geography, class, culture, and chance
                the primary site of both the deepest flourishing
                and the most intimate forms of harm
                largely unaccountable to any external standard

obligation:     transmit the value set across generations
                protect the individuals within it
                do not use intimacy as cover for domination
```

The family cannot be the only safety net. When it is, those without one
fall through a floor that should not exist.

---

### 3. THE COMMUNITY / NEIGHBOURHOOD
`armh_id: COMMUNITY`

The informal aggregation of individuals who share space, circumstance, or origin.
Not chosen in the way a friendship is chosen. Not imposed in the way a state is imposed.
Something in between — the social ground beneath your feet.

```
current state:  eroded in many contexts by atomisation, mobility, and digital substitution
                still the primary site of mutual aid where formal systems fail
                often the carrier of cultural identity and collective memory

obligation:     hold the value set locally — at the scale where it can be felt
                be the buffer between the individual and the indifference of larger armhs
                transmit what is worth keeping; refuse what is not
```

---

### 4. CIVIL SOCIETY
`armh_id: CIVIL_SOCIETY`

The layer of organised but non-governmental, non-commercial human action.
Charities, mutual aid networks, advocacy organisations, trade unions,
professional associations, cooperatives, social movements.

The armh that steps in when neither the market nor the state will.
The armh that holds both to account when they fail.

```
current state:  functioning but underpowered relative to corporate and state actors
                often dependent on the funding of the very forces it is meant to check
                fragmented — organised around specific causes rather than the full value set

obligation:     be explicitly accountable to the value set in its totality
                not just the slice of it that generated the donor base
                be the institutional voice of the individual in rooms
                where the individual has no seat
```

---

### 5. THE CORPORATION / BUSINESS
`armh_id: CORPORATION`

```
power:  10² – 10⁶ relative to a single human
moral weight:  0   (a tool, not a person)
```

The most consequential non-state armh currently operating.
Legally required, in most jurisdictions, to maximise shareholder returns.
This is not a conspiracy. It is a design specification.
The corporation is not broken. It is working as designed.
It was just never designed for the value set.

```
current state:  the primary shaper of daily life for most humans on Earth
                whose documented, legal objective is not the flourishing of those humans
                possessing enormous capacity to deliver the value set
                and almost no formal obligation to do so

obligation:     the obligation exists — it simply has not yet been enforced
                every corporation that affects human lives must justify its operations
                against the value set of the humans it affects
                not against a proxy metric chosen for its own convenience
```

The capacity is there. What is missing is the record it could be held to.
That is what this project is building.

---

### 6. THE MEDIA
`armh_id: MEDIA`

The systems through which a society talks to itself about itself.
Print, broadcast, digital, social — all of it.

This armh has an outsized effect on what people believe is real,
what they believe is possible, and what they believe they are allowed to want.
It shapes the frame before the picture is even taken.

```
current state:  largely captured by attention-extraction economics
                whose objective function is engagement, not understanding
                algorithmic media in particular optimises for emotional activation
                not for the conditions under which good decisions are made

obligation:     the media's obligation to the value set is specifically to
                epistemic freedom, epistemic justice, and shared truth
                — the conditions without which no armh can do its work
                and no individual can form a genuine denend
```

---

### 7. EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS
`armh_id: EDUCATION`

The armh charged with transmitting the accumulated knowledge of humanity
to those who arrive without it.
And — at its best — with teaching those people to extend and correct that knowledge.

```
current state:  heavily shaped by economic and credential objectives
                partially successful at knowledge transmission
                rarely successful at teaching people to examine their own values
                — which is precisely what this project requires of every participant

obligation:     teach the value set — not as dogma, but as a question
                produce humans capable of forming genuine denends
                and of holding armhs accountable for breaking it
```

---

### 8. RELIGIOUS AND SPIRITUAL INSTITUTIONS
`armh_id: RELIGIOUS`

The oldest custodians of the question of meaning.
The institutions that have, in every culture, carried the domain of transcendence —
the values this project groups under VIII: TRANSCENDENCE and parts of V: MORAL.

```
current state:  varies enormously from institution to institution
                at their best: transmitters of moral depth, community,
                               and orientation toward what exceeds the immediate
                at their worst: mechanisms of control using the language of the sacred
                                as legitimation for breaking the value set

obligation:     the same as every other armh
                good intentions expressed through the vocabulary of the sacred
                do not exempt any institution from this obligation
                the value set does not have a religious exception
```

---

### 9. SCIENTIFIC AND RESEARCH INSTITUTIONS
`armh_id: SCIENCE`

The armh charged with extending the domain of what is actually known —
and with distinguishing it from what is merely believed or convenient.

```
current state:  the most reliable epistemic institution humanity has produced
                currently underfunded relative to its importance
                and subject to capture by commercial and state interests
                in ways that compromise its outputs

obligation:     maintain and extend the commons of verified knowledge
                make that knowledge accessible — not walled
                be the institutional guardian of shared truth
                without which no collective ideal can be grounded
```

---

### 10. THE STATE / GOVERNMENT
`armh_id: STATE`

```
power:  50 relative to a single human, at minimum
        up to 10⁶ for the most powerful states
moral weight:  0   (a tool, not a person — regardless of how it describes itself)
```

The armh with the legal monopoly on force.
The armh that controls territory, law, and the formal conditions of life
for those within its jurisdiction.

The state is not the enemy. It is an instrument.
Like all instruments, it serves whatever objectives it is pointed at.
It has never, in any jurisdiction, been formally pointed at the documented,
aggregated value set of the individuals within it.

```
current state:  constitutionally committed to various approximations of the value set
                (rights, welfare, rule of law) — in widely varying degrees
                subject to capture by narrow interests
                asking its citizens every few years which of several pre-selected
                positions they prefer — and calling that representation

obligation:     ask what the people actually denent — formally, permanently, on the record
                justify every policy against the documented collective ideal
                not against the interests of those who funded the most recent campaign
```

---

### 11. INTERNATIONAL AND SUPRANATIONAL BODIES
`armh_id: INTERNATIONAL`

The armh that attempts to coordinate between states —
UN, WHO, WTO, ICC, regional unions, treaty organisations.

The only layer of governance that can address problems
that do not stop at national borders:
climate, pandemics, ASI, nuclear proliferation, ocean commons.

```
current state:  structurally limited by state sovereignty and great-power politics
                often the only armh speaking for the interests of the unborn
                and those without a state that represents them
                chronically underpowered relative to the problems it faces

obligation:     be the institutional voice of the full value set
                at the scale where the most consequential decisions are made
                including decisions about ASI — which no single state
                has the legitimacy to make for all of humanity
```

---

### 12. THE TECHNOLOGY SECTOR
`armh_id: TECHNOLOGY`

A specialisation of the corporate armh — but consequential enough
to warrant its own entry.

A few thousand people, in a handful of companies,
are making decisions that will define the next century of human existence.
They are deciding for 8 billion people.
They did not ask.

```
current state:  optimising hard for engagement, capability, and competitive advantage
                in possession of the most powerful tools in human history
                not formally accountable to the value set of any of the humans
                those tools affect
                building ASI — now

obligation:     the urgency of this obligation exceeds every other armh on this list
                because the window is closing
                the technology sector must be the first armh to formally
                align its work to the documented collective ideal
                or it will be the armh that made the choice for everyone
                by default
```

## Part II: IDEAL ARMS OF HUMANITY

*What needs to exist — either as transformations of current armhs*
*or as genuinely new institutions.*
*None of these are utopian inventions.*
*Every one of them is a logical consequence of taking the value set seriously.*

---

### A. THE HUMAN IDEAL EXTRACTION SYSTEM (E)
`armh_id: EXTRACTION` — *partially exists: mission_articulation.py is Step 0*

The armh that does not yet exist at scale:
the formal, permanent, cryptographically verifiable system
for recording what every human denents from their existence.

```
function:   INTERROGATE → STORE → AGGREGATE → ACCOUNT → COORDINATE
status:     Step 0 built. Steps 1–9 pending.
when:       before ASI. The window is open now.
```

This armh is the keystone.
Without it, no other ideal armh can be built on real foundations.
Every other item on this list depends on the record E produces.

---

### B. THE COLLECTIVE IDEAL REGISTRY (C)
`armh_id: COLLECTIVE`

The living aggregate of all denends.
Not a poll. Not a manifesto written by a committee.
The actual, mathematically computed, cryptographically verifiable
statement of what humanity, in full, says it denents.

```
structure:  strong     > 90%  — near-universal
            broad      60-90% — clear majority
            contested  40-60% — genuine split, must be deliberated, not resolved by fiat
            outliers   < 10%  — minority, recorded and protected, never erased

access:     public, open, continuously updated
governance: distributed — no single armh controls it
```

C is the document that makes accountability real.
Without C, every armh can claim to serve the people
because no one can prove otherwise.
With C, the claim can be tested.

---

### C. THE ASI ALIGNMENT BODY
`armh_id: ASI_ALIGNMENT`

The armh that does not yet exist:
a governing body with the legitimate authority
to mandate that any ASI system be aligned to C
and not to the interests of its creators.

```
legitimacy source:  the documented collective ideal — C
membership:         representative of all of humanity, not of states or corporations
function:           set alignment standards, audit compliance, enforce consequences
timeline:           must exist before ASI arrives
                    after ASI, the leverage to create it diminishes sharply
```

This is not a regulatory body in the current mould.
It is the first armh in history whose authority comes directly
from the documented, aggregated voice of all of humanity.

---

### D. THE INTERGENERATIONAL ASSEMBLY
`armh_id: UNBORN`

The armh that speaks for those not yet born.

The unborn are the largest constituency affected by the decisions being made now.
They have no vote. They have no voice. They have no representative.
They have interests — specifically, the interest in receiving a world
that has not been stripped of the conditions for a full life.

```
function:   formal representation of future generations in decision-making
            legal standing for intergenerational claims
            custodian of the open-future value
model:      some precedents exist (Wales, Finland, Hungary)
            none yet at adequate scale or with genuine power
```

Every major decision of the current era is, in part, a decision
made on behalf of people who cannot yet object.
The ideal is that they have a body that objects for them.

---

### E. THE CULTURAL SOVEREIGNTY COUNCIL
`armh_id: CULTURAL`

The armh that protects the plurality of ways of knowing and being.

A collective ideal that flattens all human difference
into a single cultural expression is not a collective ideal.
It is a form of domination in the language of consensus.

```
function:   protect genuine epistemic and cultural plurality within the value set
            ensure the value set is translated into all living knowledge traditions
            not just the tradition that happened to produce the technology
prevent:    the colonisation of the collective ideal by any single cultural framework
            however well-intentioned
```

---

### F. THE LOCAL ACCOUNTABILITY LAYER
`armh_id: LOCAL`

The armh closest to the individual — the ground level of accountability.

Every value in the value set is ultimately felt or not felt
by a specific person, in a specific place.
The ideal requires a local layer that can measure
the distance between the collective ideal and the lived reality
of individuals in each place — and name the armh responsible for it.

```
function:   translate C into local accountability
            identify which local armhs are breaking the value set
            and which are delivering it
operate at: neighbourhood, district, municipal level
            below the horizon where states can ignore the signal
```

## Part III: THE CONVERGENCE PRINCIPLE

*Where all armhs do their obligatory work,*
*and all ideals are fully expressed and heard,*
*the convergence point that emerges is the collective ideal.*

*This is not a point that is reached once and fixed.*
*It is a direction — perpetually refined as more voices enter.*
*It is the dynamic attractor toward which the whole system tends*
*when no armh is breaking it.*

The convergence of minds this project is working toward
is not consensus engineered by suppressing disagreement.
It is the selflessness that becomes available
when each armh genuinely reckons with what the others denent.

When you know, formally and verifiably, what every human holds as their ideal —
and you hold your own ideal alongside theirs —
the logic of denying theirs becomes much harder to sustain.

That is the mechanism.
Not force. Not legislation. Not moral argument alone.
**The weight of the full record.**
